Email Script

This script has the ability to send emails to large numbers of people.

In the template section, specify a template. The following is a good example of a template

To: @@@email@@@
From: me@me.com
Subject: Hello, @@@firstname@@@! Would you like to volunteer with us?
Cc: them@them.com
Bcc: boss@them.com
Reply-to: us@organization.com
###
Hello: 

I thought you might be interested in volunteering with us!  

Just reply to this email to let us know if you are intested. 

Best.
